What the Kids of Angelina Jolie and Brad Pitt Look Like Now

July 2024 · 3 minute read

1. Maddox Chivan

Jolie adopted her first child from an orphanage in Battambang, Cambodia, in 2002, when he was only 7 months old. 4 years later, he was also adopted by Brad Pitt and changed his surname from “Jolie” to “Jolie-Pitt.” Now, he’s already 22 years old, and he’s been studying at Yonsei University in Seoul, South Korea, since 2019.

2. Pax Thien

Jolie adopted 3-year-old Pax in 2007 from an orphanage in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. The next year, Pitt adopted the boy as well. Today, he’s 19 years old, he’s interested in photography and also played a minor role in Maleficent.

3. Zahara Marley

Zahara was adopted by Jolie from an orphanage in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, in 2005. She was 6 months old at the time. Brad Pitt adopted Zahara the next year. For now, she’s 18, and she already has a couple of film credits, but her dream is to become a supermodel.

4. Shiloh Nouvel

CALA / Broadimage / East News, Tiziana Fabi / AFP / East News

In order to welcome their first biological child, Jolie and Pitt traveled to Namibia, where Jolie gave birth to her daughter in a city named Swakopmund. Today, Shiloh is 17 years old and is already considered an LGBTQ+ idol.
