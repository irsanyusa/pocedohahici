The Masked Singer rounded out the season with a talented new group of vocalists. From the adorable Baby Mammoth to the creepy Jack in the Box to the regal Prince, each one brought their own unique personality. But maybe the most impressive was the Prince’s performance and voice. This frog can SING!

So who could be the Prince? We’ll break down all the clues and make our celebrity prediction!

loading="lazy"

Clues on The Masked Singer Season 7

The Prince’s clues were all set up like an 80’s workout video. All the Men in Black are wearing colorful, short shorts and headbands. His clue-mercial is for a “TOAD-AL BODY WORKOUT.” We see a model of Mars in the clues, while Prince says “I’ve been working my froggy legs off my whole life.” The Men in Black toss a squeaking teddy bear between them.

Prince claims his signature moves are the Lily Pad Leap and the Reach and Bend. We see a big diamond ring on a finger, then someone doing jazz hands and a leg workout. The workout tape is “18 easy payments of $20.06.” Other clues we’ve seen are a shoutout by Jamie Lee Curtis, and a blue letterman jacket with a gold “30” on it.

The Prince gave an additional clue after his performance, saying “I’ve performed in front of many people before many times.” He also mentions, “I’m happy to be back on a team and I hope to knock it out of the park.”

Popular Guesses for The Masked Singer Prince

Ken predicted Prince could be Enrique Iglesias, which Nicole quickly agreed with. Robin went off the athlete tip, guessing baseball player Derek Jeter. Jenny thought it could be Alex Rodriguez based on the clues. The other judges build off her guess, mentioning that he won the World Series in 2006.

Online, fans are definitely liking the judges’ guesses of Enrique Iglesias and ARod. Some of the other top predictions are Marc Anthony, Zac Efron, Billy Blanks, or a Broadway star like Corbin Bleu, Cheyenne Jackson or Jeremy Jordan.

Who is the Prince on The Masked Singer?

Listening to the clues and performance, this one fell together pretty easily. Everything just makes sense, and we’re excited to see this celebrity finally get the recognition he deserves for that amazing voice.

The Prince is…

Cheyenne Jackson!

That voice and ability to act screams Broadway and all the clues line up perfectly. Let’s dive in!

The fact that all of his clues were a nod to the 80’s could’ve been a reference to his role in the original cast of the musical Xanadu. The Mars clue points to his appearance in the show Life on Mars early in his career. A diamond ring in the clues is referring to his role as Johnny Diamond in an episode of American Housewife. The 2006 clue also makes sense, as that’s the year Cheyenne appeared in the Academy Award nominated movie United 93.

And the other clues we saw also fit with Cheyenne Jackson. Prince said he’s “happy to be back on a team” and on Glee, he played the coach of an acapella group. The “30” on a letterman jacket could be a nod to his recurring role on 30 Rock. And the shoutout from Jamie Lee Curtis? The two are starring in a movie called Borderlands set to come out later this year.

And if all that weren’t enough, Cheyenne Jackson recently appeared on another FOX reality show hosted by Ken Jeong, I Can See Your Voice. Other The Masked Singer alum have performed on that show in the past, including Adrienne Bailon-Houghton, Nick Lachey, Kelly Obsourne, Donny Osmond, Bob Saget, Jesse McCartney, Katharine McPhee, Bow Wow, Jewel, Brian Austin Green, LeAnn Rimes, Margaret Cho, and Kandi Burruss. So it’s not a stretch to think he would follow in their footsteps!
