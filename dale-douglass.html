Dale Douglass (Golfer) - On This Day

July 2024 · 1 minute read

Full Name: Dale Dwight Douglass
Profession: Golfer

Biography: Won 3 PGA Tour titles;, 11 Champions Tour titles and the US Senior Open in 1986.

Born: March 5, 1936
Birthplace: Wewoka, Oklahoma, USA

Generation: Silent Generation
Chinese Zodiac: Rat
Star Sign: Pisces

Died: July 6, 2022 (aged 86)

Playing Career

  • 1986-06-29 US Senior Open Men's Golf, Scioto CC: Dale Douglass wins by 1 stroke ahead of Gary Player for his lone Champions Tour major title
  • 1987-02-15 Senior PGA Championship, PGA National GC: Chi-Chi Rodríguez wins his second and final Champions Tour major title by 1 stroke from Dale Douglass
  • 1994-04-03 The Tradition Senior Men's Golf, GC at Desert Mountain: Ray Floyd wins first of 4 Champions Tour majors with birdie on first playoff hole against Dale Douglass
  • 1997-04-20 Senior PGA Championship, PGA National GC: Defending champion Hale Irwin wins his second of 3 straight Senior PGA C’ships beating runners-up Dale Douglass and Jack Nicklaus by 12 strokes
