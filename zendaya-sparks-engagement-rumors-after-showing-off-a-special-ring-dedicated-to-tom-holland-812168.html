Zendaya Sparks Engagement Rumors After Showing Off a Special Ring Dedicated to Tom Holland

September 2024 · 2 minute read

Eagle-eyed internet users have noticed a very notable piece of jewelry on Zendaya’s hand, and now they’ve raised some questions that we’re desperate to know the answers to. Let’s learn more about how the famous couple, Zendaya and Tom Holland, have stirred the online community this time.

Gilbert Flores/Broadimage/Broad Image/East News

Tom Holland and Zendaya first crossed paths in 2017 when they starred in what we now know as the beginning of a large franchise, Spider-Man: Homecoming. The 2 played love interests over the course of 3 movies and, eventually, found their way into each other’s arms.

In 2021, the 2 co-stars were spotted kissing in LA, which marked the official start of their romance, at least in the public’s eyes. While it might’ve seemed like their relationship was a PR move ahead of the release of Spider-Man: No Way Home, the couple is still going strong in 2023. Furthermore, the 2 might be only a few steps away from taking their love to the next level.

A few days ago, Zendaya got her nails done by Instagram nail artist Marina Dobyk. Many of the actress’ fans noticed a special ring on her finger with the initials TH, which everyone assumed stands for Tom Holland. And this is not the first time Zendaya has worn the ring, as another video from Dobyk’s page featured the outstanding piece of jewelry.

Fans instantly started speculating that Zendaya and Tom are possibly very close to being engaged or, at least, so serious about each other that they’re definitely heading for a happy ending together:

  • “I love everything about this.....they mesh so perfect and are so happy. Rooting for them.” — ToniZ126 / Twitter

At this time, there is no official information about Tom and Zendaya being engaged, but we will keep an eye on this happy couple and keep you updated.
