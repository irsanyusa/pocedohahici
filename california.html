Events in Fresno History

  • 1922-03-23 KMJ-AM in Fresno CA begins radio transmissions
  • 1953-06-01 KMJ (now KSEE) TV channel 24 in Fresno, CA (NBC) begins broadcasting
  • 1953-10-01 KJEO TV channel 47 in Fresno, CA (CBS/ABC) begins broadcasting
  • 1956-05-10 KFSN TV channel 30 in Fresno, CA (ABC/CBS) begins broadcasting
  • 1958-09-18 The Fresno Drop: Bank of America mails out 60,000 BankAmericards in Fresco, California, the first credit card (later renamed VISA)
  • 1961-12-18 KAIL TV channel 53 in Fresno, CA (IND) begins broadcasting
  • 1961-12-23 KICU TV channel 43 in Visalia-Fresno, CA (IND) begins broadcasting
  • 1971-10-04 KMPH TV channel 26 in Visalia-Fresno, CA (IND) begins broadcasting
  • 1991-10-05 Fresno State ties NCAA football record with 49 points in a quarter (2nd), as they route New Mexico, 94-17 at Bulldog Stadium, Fresno
  • 1996-04-29 Howard Stern Radio Show premieres in Fresno, California on KFRR 104.1 FM
  • 2012-11-06 5 people are shot at a poultry processing plant in Fresno, California
