Stagecoach Festival 2024 Announced Their Star-Studded Lineup of Performers

August 2024 · 3 minute read

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment

Photo by Taylor Hill/FilmMagic, Photo by Axelle/Bauer-Griffin/FilmMagic

Reading Time: 3 minutes

The Stagecoach Festival, one of the most anticipated events on the country music calendar, has unveiled its 2024 lineup. From country legends to unexpected surprises, this year’s roster promises a memorable experience for festival-goers. Here’s a glimpse into what to expect from this exciting musical extravaganza set to take place from April 26 to April 28 at the Empire Polo Club in Indio, California.

Stagecoach Festival 2024 Announced Who’s Performing

Leading the pack is Morgan Wallen, making his debut as a headliner at Stagecoach. Known for his chart-topping hits and undeniable stage presence, Wallen is sure to bring fresh energy to the festival.

Eric Church and Miranda Lambert are no strangers to the Stagecoach stage, having previously headlined in 2016 and 2015, respectively. Church’s return is particularly special as he was originally scheduled to headline in 2020, a performance that was unfortunately canceled due to the COVID-19 pandemic.

Perhaps the most surprising addition to the lineup is none other than Post Malone. While the hip-hop-turned-pop sensation has displayed his love for country music in the past, his appearance at a country festival is a first. Post Malone is set to perform a unique set of “country covers,” leaving fans curious and excited.

Stagecoach 2024 also boasts an impressive roster of country veterans and emerging talents. Willie Nelson, the iconic 90-year-old country legend, will make a triumphant return to Stagecoach after a long hiatus.

Meanwhile, Jelly Roll, a rising star in the country scene, is set to take the main stage just before Eric Church on the festival’s opening night. Hardy and Bailey Zimmerman, both recent CMA nominees, are among the hot stars of the moment gracing the Stagecoach stage.

How To Secure Your Tickets For Stagecoach 2024

The Stagecoach 2024 festival is just around the corner. If you’re eager to be part of this country music experience, you’ll want to grab your tickets before they’re gone. Tickets for Stagecoach 2024 will become available for purchase on September 15, exclusively through the official Stagecoach website.

General Admission passes are your standard entry tickets to the festival. They are priced in tiers, starting at $399 with early buyers getting the advantage of lower prices. For those seeking an upgraded experience, there’s the Rhinestone Saloon Pass, priced at $799 plus fees. This pass not only grants access to a standing-room-only area near the Mane Stage but also includes entry to the Rhinestone Saloon, featuring specialty food and drink vendors, shaded seating areas, and more.

For the ultimate Stagecoach experience, VIP packages are available. These include Desert Diamond priced at $2999 plus fees, Gold Rush priced at $1999 plus fees, and Hi Ho Silver priced at $1499 plus fees. These packages come with various perks, including reserved seating options and exclusive access.

It’s worth noting that ticket prices are subject to change. Additionally, some passes may sell out quickly, so be sure to act promptly when they become available.
