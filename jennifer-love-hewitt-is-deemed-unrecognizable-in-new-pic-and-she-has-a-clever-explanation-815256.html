Jennifer Love Hewitt Is Deemed Unrecognizable in New Pic, and She Has a Clever Explanation

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Jennifer Love Hewitt’s social media was flooded with comments accusing her of plastic surgery after posting new footage. However, the stunning 44-year-old actress responded most unexpectedly to these trolls, leaving us even more at a loss for words.

A brand new look

It all started when the Ghost Whisperer actress’s hairstylist, nikkilee901, posted an Instagram clip showing her drastic hair transformation. The video showed the stunning star cutting her long dark brown tresses into a sleek bob with a trendy curtain bang. She also changed her hair color and opted for a mahogany brown.

The hairstylist captioned the post, “Was time to spice things up,” along with a red hot pepper emoji.

Gilbert Flores/Broadimage/EAST NEWS

Countless fans showered the actress with compliments about her new look. For example, one person wrote, “GORGEOUS AS ALWAYS, LOVE YOU JENNIFER,” and another added, “In love since I was a teenager!”

However, many people accused the star of having had cosmetic surgery recently and speculated whether she’d had a brow lift. Some people even described her as “unrecognizable.”

A witty response.

On Sunday, Love Hewitt responded to these comments in a clever yet playful way in her Instagram stories. She addressed those commentators, saying, “So many people said I look different,” and then added, “I look the same as always. Couldn’t look more natural. I woke up like this. Filters don’t change you that much.”

However, what was really special in her response was the way she looked in these clips. The 44-year-old used a series of filters on her face that totally altered her appearance. She added a serious note to her message: “Let people be filtered or unfiltered. Be good. Be kind. Spread love.”

We couldn’t agree more with the star’s kind message about spreading love, and we admire her for being so open and honest about her life. As she turned 44 this year, Love Hewitt opened up about feeling “insecure about aging,” which helped us relate to her even more.
