Full Name: Andrew Roy Gibb
Profession: Singer and TV Host

Biography: Andy Gibb was an English-Australian singer and actor, best known as younger brother to the Bee Gees: Barry Gibb, Robin Gibb, and Maurice Gibb.

Gibb came to fame in his own right with a series of chart-topping singles in the United States, including "I Just Want to Be Your Everything" (1977), "(Love Is) Thicker Than Water" (1977), and "Shadow Dancing" (1978).

Alongside his musical career, Gibb also performed in several stage productions including The Pirates of Penzance and Joseph and the Amazing Technicolor Dreamcoat.
