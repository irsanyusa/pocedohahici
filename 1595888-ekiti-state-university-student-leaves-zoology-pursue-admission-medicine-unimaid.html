Ekiti State University Student Abandons Her Course, Pursues Admission to Study Medicine in UNIMAID

June 2024 · 3 minute read
  • A Nigerian lady who was studying at Ekiti State University, Ado-Ekiti, decided to abandon her course of study
  • The lady said she was studying zoology at EKSU, but wanted to switch to medicine and thought she would gain admission due to her UTME score of 289
  • She applied to study medicine at the University of Maiduguri but did not get admission as she had desired

A Nigerian lady was studying at the Ekiti State University, Ado-Ekiti, but she abandoned her studies after the first semester.

She said she was studying zoology at EKSU, but that was not the course she had in mind.

The lady, Ehinola Faithfullness, said after writing the Unified Tertiary Matriculations Examination (UTME), she scored 289 and hoped to study medicine.

She abandoned the zoology course and hoped to get admission to study medicine at the University of Maiduguri.

However, Ehinola could not get the admission she desired at UNIMAID and also lost her spot at EKSU as she had missed the second semester.

She captioned her TikTok video:

"Living my life because life didn't end when I left EKSU after first semester where I was studying zoology to stay at home just because I felt I had a good JAMB score and I will be given admission in UNIMAID. But at last when the admission list came out, I was given animal and environmental biology and I couldn't go back to EKSU because I already missed second semester."

When asked what she later studied in school, Ehinola, who said the incident happened in 2017, said she studied medical laboratory science.

Lady gains admission into aviation school

Meanwhile, a lady is overwhelmed with happiness because she is about to become a student in an aviation school in Nigeria.

The lady said she would be starting her training in aviation school because her aim is to become a flight attendant.

She posted a video celebrating the fact that she had been admitted and that she would soon achieve her dreams.
