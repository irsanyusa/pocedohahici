Paris Hilton Celebrates Son Phoenixs First Halloween, and Their Costumes Are Adorable

June 2024 · 1 minute read

Paris Hilton and husband Carter Reum celebrated their baby son Phoenix’s first Halloween in style, dressing up in multiple family costumes. The couple, both 42, marked the spooky holiday with their 8-month-old son, and they shared their joy by posing for themed photos.

Baby Phoenix’s first Halloween costume

For his first Halloween, little Phoenix dressed up as a pilot, matching his famous mom, Paris Hilton. Paris rocked the iconic outfit from Britney Spears’ 2003 “Toxic” music video, complete with a teal Pan Am costume and silver boots. Baby Phoenix sported aviator sunglasses, a mini-pilot uniform, and black Nikes, creating a charming mother-son duo.

Paris Hilton’s Katy Perry-inspired costume

In another Halloween twist, Paris dressed as singer Katy Perry, giving fans a closer look at her Las Vegas residency-inspired mushroom outfit, featuring a red ensemble and a mushroom-shaped hat. To make it even more adorable, baby Phoenix joined in by dressing as a mushroom in a tiny beige onesie. The family shared joyful moments as they posed for photos, creating a heartwarming family Halloween memory.

The family also dressed as characters from Mario.

For their third costume, Paris Hilton was Princess Peach, Carter Mario, and Phoenix Luigi.

Phoenix also dressed up as little Elmo.

“My lil Elmo,” Paris captioned a series of photos of herself holding her son in her arms. Baby Phoenix can be seen wearing an Elmo onesie, smiling for photos.
